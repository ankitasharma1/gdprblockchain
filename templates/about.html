About

This is a very high level diagram of the interface we built the backend and frontend for. The core idea here is that a patient is supplied with a card that contains a UID and a private_key to read or authorize access to physicians to read encrypted medical data or to update a patient's medical record. We imagine that this card could be extended to a QR code that could be presented to a physician. This is a proof-of-concept for utilizing a system of blockchains (both public and private) to make reading, writing, removing, and transferring patient medical data more secure.
